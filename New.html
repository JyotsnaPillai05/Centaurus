About Us

We are Centaurus a platform whose aim is to better the lives of members of the LGBTQIA+ community.
Our platform connects our users with healthcare proffesionals, NGO's and other queer
communities so as to give our users the facilities they want and deserve.
Our motto is simple: Pride against Prejudice to create a society which is
inclusive of every identity on the spectrum,where humanity is above everything.
We also help you come in terms with your identity. It's hard to figure things out
like this, because you never seen what is happening to you, we bring
you with valuble and credible information using our blogs and our events
